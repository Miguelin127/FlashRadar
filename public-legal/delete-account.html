Delete Your FlashRadar Account

You can request deletion of your FlashRadar account and all associated personal data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

In the app: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.

By email: Send a request from your account email to flashradarapp@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account". We will process your request within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, email, saved favorites, flip history, and all associated personal data from our systems. Some records may be retained where required by law.
